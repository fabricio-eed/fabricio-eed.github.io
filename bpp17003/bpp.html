Bank of Portugal - PROJ 17003 - 2017/18

Context:

The cleaning, treatement and packing of euro and international notes is guaranteed by the subsidiary of Bank of Portugal-Porto. Banknote treatement complies with demanding international standards relating to environment, and health and safety, including those defined by the European Central Bank for the production of euro banknotes. Work duty is certified in accordance with the international Quality, Environmental and Safety Standards, the ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and OSHAS 18001 respectively. We are talking of a output 40 banknote per secound.

Developer and system integrator for the Bank of Portugal Porto subsidiary. The requirements and the level of proficiency and rigor are strongly necessary to ensure work efficiency (OEE), machine safety, and proof of theft.

Tasks:

Programmer of the robotic manipulator KUKA and the vision system implemented with Sherlock Vision, Cognex and IFM multicode reader .

Robot do a pick and place of wad of notes. It picks on the conveyor and place into a box (5x2). They are different size of notes so the process of pick and place dynamically adapts the work position.

The Vision system performs various tasks along the assembly line. It checks the serial number of the wad and cross check with the expect database number, confirm the labelling with success, the wad size, matches with the amount of subpacks expected and the alignment of the paper band. The Vision System logs the informations on a database and record images and system status.

Creation of a GIU to integrate on the SCADA to monitor the vision system aplication and data.
